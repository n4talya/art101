Problems

Maya and I both had issues getting our output to show on our website. It would appear in my console log when the button was pressed, but it would not show up on the actual page. So we worked with Vibha and she let us know that the results of this API was in the form of a string. Meaning that we must include result[0].answer in our javascript if we wanted it to appear on our webpage.

Reflection
