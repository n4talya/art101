Challenge

The challenge for this lab was to use conditionals and buttons to give a user a Hogwarts house based on the length of their name.

Problems

The conditionals and button portion of the lab was straightforward but for awhile I couldn't understand why the house wouldn't change when I input different names. After combing through my code I realized it's because I forgot to add the word string in my Javascript. As soon as I put that in, it was working just fine.

Reflection

I enjoyed using conditionals in this lab and it makes me excited to start using loops in the next lab. I also had fun adding a gradient to my background and just seeing how far I've come in this class.
